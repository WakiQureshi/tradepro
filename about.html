Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming a global trading platform trusted by millions

Founded in 2015 by a team of financial experts and technologists

TradePro began as a small startup with a big vision: to democratize trading and make financial markets accessible to everyone. Our founders, seasoned traders themselves, recognized the need for a platform that combined professional-grade tools with an intuitive interface.

What started as a team of five working out of a shared office space has grown into a global company with offices in New York, London, Singapore, and Tokyo, serving over 2 million clients worldwide.

Today, we continue to innovate, constantly pushing the boundaries of what's possible in online trading while staying true to our core mission of empowering traders.
